Project Idea

This is an APP for workout, more and more people realised how important to have a good healthy body, they want to workout but they don’t know what to do or how to plan, other app in market is not flexible enough to match different level of workout requirement, this app will help them to choose and with easy operation, everyone can have their own plan and motivation.

the APP will recommend exercise style and plan according to user’s goal, we have yoga lesson, cross-fit, weight lifting, pregnancy exercise , new starter, lose-weight exercise and relaxing workout. after user enter their height, weight, age, and their goal, the system will make some plan mix different type of exercise to let them choose or they can make a plan themselves, for example user can add different exercise to their cross-fit, set up exercise time and rest time of each workout, they can also link music from other APP, and count down sound effect. In yoga lesson, video of professional teacher will show the move and tell user how to do it.

The APP has 15mins podcast everyday, topics including different people’s workout experience, the science of workout, ect.

When user finished one period of workout, they will receive a report, it included the total time, lessons user took, calorie burn, average time everyday, percentage of the plan, the time user do workout most, which day user do longest workout, then ask user to enter their weight to record the changes, and begin next stage.

There are activities online every week or month, like“200 mins yoga challenge” or “workout everyday for a month challenge”. user get reward if they succeed, for example user get coupons can be use within the app. or get a yoga mat from sponsors which put AD in the APP.

The APP can be used on phone and table, both Android and Apple system, we use Java, Sketch,Adobe Experience Design and Adobe Illustration to do UI design.

Skill required UI, user interface design. basic application design principles, and scaling web based applications, frameworks, Database, testing and debugging skills, familiar with Java, React,MongoDB.

The flexiblity, both workout expert and freshman can find their own style of workout, customise details and get motivated by achieve a little goal every day. let people enjoy their healthy body and life.
